My Books

What is this?

This is a list of all of the books I have read. This list does not include any of the multitude of textbooks that I read during my undergraduate at the University of Nebraska Omaha; that is to say, these are books I read during my free time and out of my interest.

Why is this here?

I put this list on here to accentuate my enthusiasm towards and interest in programming and other job-related domains. I would encourage anyone interested in the software development industry to consider reading these books, as they can teach you a great many things about software development, programming, and people.

The books

Programming related

  • Effective C++ by Eric Meyers
  • More Effective C++ by Eric Meyers
  • Design Patterns by the Gang of Four
  • The Pragmatic Programmer by Andrew Hunt and David Thomas
  • The Mythical Man-Month by Fred Brooks
  • Eric Meyer On CSS by Eric Meyer
  • UML Distilled by Martin Fowler
  • Understanding .NET by David Chappell (not the comedian!)

Non-programming related

  • How to Win Friends and Influence People by Dale Carnegie
  • Talking the Winner's Way by Leil Lowndes
  • Think and Grow Rich by Napolean Hill
  • Comedy Writing Secrets by Melvin Helitzer
